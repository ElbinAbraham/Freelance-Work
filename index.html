OUR STORY

Why we started it?

Ajay Export World is a global export company founded by buzzing entrepreneur Ajay Parswani in pursuit of fulfilling the needs of a growing society. With over 5 years experience in the industry, Ajay began his journey as a gold jewellery manufacturer in 1988 under the name DBS Jewels. working as a financial advisor from 5 years Ajay reignited his passion for exports and established Ajay Export World. At Ajay Export World, our mission is to serve clients from around the world by offering a wide range of high-quality products. We are dedicated to meeting the unique needs and demands of our clients, and continuously strive to improve our services. Our product catalogue continues to expand as we source the best quality goods from various markets to ensure we provide the best products at the most competitive prices. As a global export company, we believe in building long-lasting relationships with our clients, suppliers, and partners. We take pride in our excellence that our customers have come to expect from us. We are committed to providing the best products and services to our clients, and we are always looking for ways to improve our services.
